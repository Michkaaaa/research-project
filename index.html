Essential Question

The essential question below guided my research during this project.

What are the attributes of a quality volleyball player?

Quility volleyball player?

Learn and master basic skills (serving, passing, setting, spiking, blocking, digging). Incorporate strength, agility, and endurance training. Develop a positive attitude and focus on mental preparation. Communicate and strategize effectively with your team. Practice regularly with a variety of drills and matches.

These three Physical Elements of Performance help young athletes develop the proper strength, coordination, agility and quickness needed to excel in the sport of volleyball.

Zehra Gunes

Born on July 7, 1999 in Istanbul Kartal, Zehra Güneş started playing volleyball at VakıfBank at the age of 12. Two years in a row, she won both the Stars and Youth Championships in Turkey.

Currently, she plays for Vakıfbank Istanbul and is a member of the Turkey women's national volleyball team.
